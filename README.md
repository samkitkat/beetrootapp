# 🌱 Beetroot App

This app is a gentle, private space to track your cycle, mood, and daily experiences.

## ✨ About

Designed to feel calm, simple, and intentional.

Instead of overwhelming charts or cluttered data, it focuses on:
- cycle awareness
- daily journaling
- emotional + physical patterns
- privacy-first tracking

Everything is stored locally on your device.

## 🔒 Privacy

Your data is:
- stored locally on your device
- never uploaded
- never shared
- deleted if you uninstall the app
